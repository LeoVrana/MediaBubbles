Democrats on the House Intelligence Committee released a trove of screenshots showing Russian-linked ads, events, memes, pages, and petitions that had been disseminated across Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. The content appears aimed at further inflaming recent debates in America. Some targeted issues like immigration that came up repeatedly during the campaign. Others focused on Bill and Hillary Clinton. The release came as officials from Facebook, Twitter, and Google testified before Congress about Russia-linked actors using their platforms to attempt to influence the 2016 presidential election.
