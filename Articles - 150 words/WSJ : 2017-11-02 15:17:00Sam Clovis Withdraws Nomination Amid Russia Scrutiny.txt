WASHINGTON—Former Trump campaign aide Sam Clovis withdrew himself from consideration for a top post at the Department of Agriculture on Thursday amid questions about his involvement with a onetime campaign adviser’s interactions with Russians ahead of the 2016 election. Mr Clovis, a former conservative talk radio host in Iowa, had been under consideration for the position of undersecretary for research, education and economics at the Agriculture Department. The White House said the withdrawal was Mr Clovis’s choice. ...
