The long-awaited Republican proposal to overhaul the tax code is now competing with special counsel Robert Mueller’s investigation into potential ties between President Trump’s campaign and Russia. GOP lawmakers insist the jolt to Washington from Mueller’s probe doesn’t hurt their efforts to rewrite the tax code. The House Ways and Means Committee is set to release their tax bill on Wednesday, and GOP leaders have said they hope legislation passes Congress by Thanksgiving. They got a boost last week when the House approved a budget resolution that allows the tax bill to avoid a Democratic filibuster.
