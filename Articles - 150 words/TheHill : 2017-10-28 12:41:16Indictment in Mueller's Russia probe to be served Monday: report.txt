An indictment in special counsel Robert Mueller’s investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 election will reportedly be handed down on Monday. NBC News reported the development on Saturday, citing a US official familiar with the matter. A spokesman for the special counsel's office declined to comment to The Hill.
