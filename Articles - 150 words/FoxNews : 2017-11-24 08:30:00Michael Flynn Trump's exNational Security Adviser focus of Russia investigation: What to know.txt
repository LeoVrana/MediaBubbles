Lawyers for Michael Flynn, President Trump’s former national security adviser, have reportedly said they will no longer communicate with the White House legal team – a move that could signal his cooperation with the special counsel’s investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 election. Flynn’s legal team informed the White House lawyers of their decision last week, according to the Associated Press. The decision could be a sign that Flynn is moving to cooperate with special counsel Robert Mueller's investigation or negotiate a deal for himself.
