Donald Trump made good on a campaign promise this week by officially recognizing Jerusalem as Israel’s capital and beginning the process of moving the US Embassy to the Israeli city. The president received praise and criticism for the move. However, there is one outlet which wrote a headline no one was expecting. CNN published a story entitled, ‘Donald Trump — keeper of promises.’ In the article, CNN begrudgingly admits that Trump has bucked the typical political norms and kept most of his campaign promises. The need to live up to that image helps explain why Trump, who is under ever-increasing pressure from the Russia investigation, on Wednesday recognized Jerusalem as Israel’s capital despite the widely acknowledged risks.
