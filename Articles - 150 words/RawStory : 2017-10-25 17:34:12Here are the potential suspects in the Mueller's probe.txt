It has been a big few days in the ongoing investigation into Russia’s efforts to influence the 2016 election and potentially collude with Donald Trump’s campaign. President Trump’s personal lawyer, Michael Cohen, has appeared before multiple congressional committees, and the Hillary Clinton campaign was reported to be one of the financers behind an explosive dossier alleging links between Trump and Russia.
