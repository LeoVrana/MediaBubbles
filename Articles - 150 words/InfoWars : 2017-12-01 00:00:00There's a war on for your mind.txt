As news broke that Michael Flynn would testify that Donald Trump ordered him to talk to the Russians during the transition phase between administrations, the left exploded with glee that actual evidence of “Russian collusion” was in sight and it could bring down Trump. The reality turned out to be somewhat disappointing for them. ABC News reported that Trump directed Flynn to speak with the Russians “initially as a way to work together to fight ISIS in Syria.” In other words, Trump committed the grievous sin of attempting to work diplomatically with another super power to defeat Islamic terrorism. This is hardly the damaging revelation Trump’s enemies had been hoping for. “Sorry to be a party-pooper but if this is the extent of the “directive” Flynn received, it’s incredibly mundane,” commented journalist Michael Tracey.
