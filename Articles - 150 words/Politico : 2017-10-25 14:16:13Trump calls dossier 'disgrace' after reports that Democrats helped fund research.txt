Trump calls dossier 'disgrace' after reports that Democrats helped fund research President Donald Trump on Wednesday called a now-famous dossier on alleged ties between his 2016 campaign and the Russian government a “disgrace” to Democrats and hinted that he knows the identity of a Republican who initially paid for the research during the GOP primary. Hillary Clinton’s presidential campaign and the Democratic National Committee helped fund research that led to the dossier, news organizations reported Tuesday, though the research initially was paid for by an as-yet unnamed Republican. The final product, which contains salacious allegations against the president, has become part of ongoing congressional probes into Russian meddling in the 2016 election.
