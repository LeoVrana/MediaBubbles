President Trump’s former longtime bodyguard Keith Schiller reportedly testified to Congress this week that a Russian offered to “send five women” to Trump’s hotel room on his 2013 visit to Moscow but was turned down. NBC News reported Thursday that three sources with knowledge of Schiller’s testimony said that he told House Intelligence Committee investigators about the offer, which came from a Russian participant in a business meeting that Trump attended before the 2013 Miss Universe pageant. Schiller allegedly told House investigators that the offer was declined and that he responded by saying “We don’t do that type of stuff.” The former White House aide testified that he and Trump laughed about the matter.
