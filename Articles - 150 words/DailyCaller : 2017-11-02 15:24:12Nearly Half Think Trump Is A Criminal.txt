REUTERS/Kevin Lamarque Nearly half of Americans believe that President Donald Trump committed a crime “in connection with possible Russian attempts to influence the election,” according to a poll released Thursday. The Washington Post/ABC poll also found that 58 percent of Americans approve of how Special Counsel Robert Mueller is conducting his probe into alleged Russian election interference. The investigation has so far charged three former Trump campaign aides. Two of the aides, Paul Manafort and Rick Gates, were charged with financial crimes, while former foreign policy adviser George Papadopoulos pleaded guilty to lying to federal officials about his ties to individuals with alleged connections to the Russian government.
