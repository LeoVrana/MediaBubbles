Surprise! Soon after Mr Manafort and Mr Gates turned themselves in, newly unsealed court documents revealed that another Trump campaign adviser, George Papadopoulos, pleaded guilty in early October to lying to the F.B.I. about conversations he had with a professor who had substantial ties to Russian government officials. The professor introduced Mr Papadopoulos to a woman he claimed was a relative of President Vladimir Putin of Russia. Then in April 2016 he told Mr Papadopoulos that Moscow had “dirt” on Hillary Clinton in the form of “thousands of emails.” Mr Papadopoulos tried for several months to arrange a meeting between the Trump campaign and the Russians, including Mr Putin.
