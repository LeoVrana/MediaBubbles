Multiple congressional committees are investigating an Obama-era deal that resulted in a Russian company purchasing American uranium mines. Lawmakers are also asking the Department of Justice to lift a reported gag order on a confidential informant who is expected to have more information about the agreement that allowed Russia to control about one-fifth of the uranium mining in the US – and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton’s involvement in it.
