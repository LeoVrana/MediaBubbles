Jake Tapper (Photo: screen capture) CNN’s Jake Tapper on Monday laid out all of the evidence (so far) that led to the indictments of two former Donald Trump associates and guilty plea from a third. “How did we get here?” Tapper asked, following a deluge of news reports surrounding special counsel Robert Mueller’s investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 presidential election and possible collusion with the Trump campaign. The CNN host went on to “briefly review” what reporters have learned “about Russia, the DNC and Clinton emails and the Trump team.” “It comes in four stages,” Tapper explained. “There’s the hack, the dangle, the fishing and then the release.” Tapper noted that in July 2015 and March 2016, Russian hackers breached the DNC and stole a massive number of emails.
