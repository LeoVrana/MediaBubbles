Former CIA Director John Brennan said Sunday it’s “worrisome” that foreign leaders are able to manipulate President Donald Trump by playing on his ego and insecurities. Brennan blasted Trump Sunday on CNN’s “State of the Union” for attempting to downplay the conclusions reached by the US intelligence community, namely that Russia interfered in the 2016 presidential election for the purpose of backing Trump.
