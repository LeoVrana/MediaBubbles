The New York Times reports that Manafort and former business associate Rick Gates are being hit with federal charges as a result of Mueller’s probe. CNN is reporting that Manafort has agreed to turn himself in on Monday. Manafort was spotted leaving his home by Washington, D.C. local news station WUSA 9 being driven by his attorney.
