Republicans mounted a counterattack this week against the drumbeat from multiple investigations into Moscow’s meddling in the 2016 election, seizing on revelations of Democratic ties to a now-famous dossier on Donald Trump’s alleged Russian connections to cast doubt on the probes. In a dizzying 24 hours, congressional Republicans announced two investigations into old controversies that centered on 2016 Democratic presidential nominee Hillary Clinton. And the news that Clinton’s camp helped fund the research behind the disputed dossier sent Trump allies into a fury — and accelerated the rising tensions on Capitol Hill.
