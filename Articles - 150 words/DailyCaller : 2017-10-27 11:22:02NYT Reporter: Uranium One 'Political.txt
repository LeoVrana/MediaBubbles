New York Times reporter Eric Lipton referred to the recent coverage surrounding former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton’s involvement in the Uranium One scandal as a “tactical political strategy” in a Thursday tweet. Lipton’s employer, The New York Times, reported in 2015 that the Obama administration approved the acquisition of the Canadian mining company Uranium One by a Russian-backed firm. The 2010 merger gave the Russian firm Rosatom access to roughly 20 percent of American uranium deposits. The story re-emerged after a bombshell report from the Hill, which revealed the FBI had gathered extensive evidence prior to the deal’s approval that the Kremlin compromised employees within an American uranium trucking firm in an effort to expand Russian access to US uranium.
