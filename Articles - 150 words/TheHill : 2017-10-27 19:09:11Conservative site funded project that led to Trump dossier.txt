A conservative publication set in motion the research that led to a dossier that includes unverified claims regarding ties between President Trump and Russia, the publication revealed on Friday. The Washington Free Beacon originally funded the project through the firm Fusion GPS, a connection the publication’s lawyers revealed for the first time to the House Intelligence Committee on Friday. The Washington Examiner first reported the connection and the Free Beacon then confirmed it. The publication stopped funding the project in the spring of 2016.
