Bernstein/File Photo Special counsel Robert Mueller is questioning witnesses about a September 2016 meeting between former National Security Advisor Michael Flynn and GOP Rep Dana Rohrabacher, in connection with his investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 presidential election. NBC News reports that Flynn and Rohrabacher huddled in Washington on Sept. 20, 2016 with several of Flynn’s business associates from the Flynn Intel Group, a private security firm. Rohrabacher is a veteran Republican congressman who advocates for friendlier ties with Russia.
