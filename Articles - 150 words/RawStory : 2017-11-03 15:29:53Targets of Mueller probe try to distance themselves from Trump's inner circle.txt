A source who is currently being questioned as part of special counsel Robert Mueller’s ongoing investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 presidential election and collusion with the Trump campaign said “it’s every man for himself” when it comes to the probe, CBS reports. According to CBS, the particular source once steadfastly dismissed any coordination between Trump’s campaign and Russia. Now, he appears to have changed that tune. “I do believe the Russians interfered or tried to interfere,” the source—who maintains his innocence—admitted.
