The private investigation firm at the center of controversy over the so-called Trump dossier has settled a legal battle with the House intelligence committee over that panel's efforts to gain access to the company's bank records. Fusion GPS appealed to a federal judge in Washington last week to block a wide-ranging subpoena the House panel issued to TD Bank, seeking information on Fusion's financial transactions over a period of more than two years.
