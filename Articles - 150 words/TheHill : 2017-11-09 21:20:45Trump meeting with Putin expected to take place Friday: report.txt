President Trump's anticipated meeting with Russian President Vladimir Putin at the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Summit (APEC) will likely take place Friday, a Kremlin spokesman said, according to ABC News. The Kremlin has said that the “likelihood is great” that Trump will sit down with his Russian counterpart, although the specific details of such a meeting are still being worked out. Trump told reporters last week that he would likely meet with Putin at the conference. Friday's expected meeting is not on the president's public schedule, ABC News noted. "I think it's expected we'll meet with Putin, yeah," Trump told reporters while departing for the 13-day trip to Asia last week. The trip is Trump's longest foreign trip so far as president and includes stops in Japan, South Korea, China, Vietnam and the Philippines.
