(Photo: REUTERS/Jorge Silva) President Donald Trump and Russian President Vladimir Putin spoke over the phone for more than an hour Tuesday, according to the White House. The White House said that the two leaders spoke about defeating the Islamic State in Syria and “peacefully” resolving the continued civil war in the Middle Eastern nation. Putin and Trump also spoke about “the importance of fighting terrorism together throughout the Middle East and Central Asia,” according the White House. The statement added that they discussed implementing “lasting peace in Ukraine,” which Russia annexed land from, and continuing pressure on North Korea’s nuclear program.
