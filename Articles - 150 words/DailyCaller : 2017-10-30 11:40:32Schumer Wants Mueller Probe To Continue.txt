(Photo By Tom Williams/CQ Roll Call) WASHINGTON — Senate Minority Leader Chuck Schumer Monday wants to see the Russia investigation of Special Counsel Robert Mueller to continue following the 12-count indictment against former Trump campaign manager Paul Manafort. “These reported indictments show that the special counsel’s probe is ongoing in a very serious way. The rule of law is paramount in America and the investigation must be allowed to proceed unimpeded,” Schumer said in a written statement. “The president must not, under any circumstances, interfere with the special counsel’s work in any way.
