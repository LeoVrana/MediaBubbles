CNN legal analyst Jeffrey Toobin announced Tuesday his plans for an upcoming book focused on special counsel Robert Mueller's investigation into possible collusion between Trump campaign staff and Russia during the 2016 presidential campaign. Toobin, who also writes for The New Yorker, made the announcement on Twitter on Tuesday. "Pleased to announce I will be writing a book for @doubledaybooks about the Mueller and other Trump investigations. Advice/tips are welcome!" Toobin tweeted.
