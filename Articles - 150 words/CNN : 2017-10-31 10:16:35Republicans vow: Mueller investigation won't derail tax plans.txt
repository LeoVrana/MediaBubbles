(CNN) News of the first indictments in the investigation by Justice Department special counsel Robert Mueller captivated Capitol Hill on Monday night, just as Republicans were putting the finishing touches on their long-awaited tax bill. "It's too early. It depends on how the President reacts," said Sen Jeff Flake, a Republican from Arizona, when asked how the latest developments would affect tax reform.
