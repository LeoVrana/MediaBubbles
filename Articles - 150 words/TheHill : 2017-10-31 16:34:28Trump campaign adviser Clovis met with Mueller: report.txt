A former adviser to the Trump campaign who has emerged as a key figure in the Russia investigation met with special counsel Robert Mueller and his investigative team, NBC is reporting. Sam Clovis, whom President Trump has tapped to be the science adviser to the Department of Agriculture, was questioned by Mueller and his team last week, according to the report. Questions have been swirling around Clovis since the Justice Department revealed Monday that George Papadopoulos, a low-level foreign policy adviser to the Trump campaign, pleaded guilty to charges that he lied to federal investigators about his contacts with Russians during the campaign. Mueller is leading a Justice Department investigation into Russian meddling in the 2016 presidential election, including any possible ties between Moscow and members of Trump's campaign.
