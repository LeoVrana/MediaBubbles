(CNN) Former national security adviser Michael Flynn's decision to plead guilty to a single count of lying to the FBI over conversations he had with the Russian ambassador during the 2016 election is an absolutely massive moment in the ongoing special counsel probe being led by Robert Mueller -- and has potentially huge ramifications for President Donald Trump. Flynn's decision to cop a plea and cooperate with investigators -- coupled with the fact that the lone charge against him deals with lying to the FBI as opposed to his dealings with Turkey -- suggests that Mueller believes there could be someone higher in the chain of command than Flynn about whom the former national security adviser can provide critical information.
