This is a screenshot from The Daily Caller News Foundation's "Alternative Facts" in December 2017. (Photo: Screenshot) This week’s episode of Alternative Facts discussed a false CNN report that claimed Donald Trump Jr. obtained access to a cache of Democratic National Committee documents via WikiLeaks, a Russian-aligned activist group. CNN’s story was disseminated widely across other media platforms, including MSNBC and CBS, before the network corrected the false reporting. The documents in question were publicly available by the time they reached the Trump campaign, which belied early claims the exchange was proof of secret collusion with Russian interests. The network says the error was made in good faith, and that the story was subject to a lengthy editorial process.
