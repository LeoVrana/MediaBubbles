Of all the alleged contacts between the Trump administration and foreign governments we’ve learned about in the past year, arguably the most egregious and brazen one had nothing to do with Russia. Joshua Keating Joshua Keating is a staff writer at Slate focusing on international affairs and author of the forthcoming book, Invisible Countries.
