MONTGOMERY, Alabama — Kayla Moore, wife of Alabama Republican US Senate hopeful Roy Moore, addressed supporters and members of the media at a Women for Trump rally on the steps of the Alabama State Capitol on Friday. Moore, who had also said her husband would not quit the campaign amid allegations of sexual misconduct decades ago, noted that the “liberal press” had put much of focus on Alabama.
