One weekend in early June, President Donald Trump tested out his golf course diplomacy with Sen. Bob Corker, making the Tennessee Republican one of his first congressional partners at his Northern Virginia country club. The pair shared a cart and partnered up in a match that included former NFL quarterback Peyton Manning. They discussed both politics and policy — “a little of it all,” Corker recalled in an interview earlier this week — and there wasn’t much in the way of the trash talking that Trump is known for on the green.
